INTRODUCING GTS

McLaren’s bold fusion of performance and practicality. The GTS is sculpted to combine supercar exhilaration with everyday usability, redefining what a grand tourer can be. Its sleek silhouette is shaped by aerodynamics, delivering elegance without compromise. Inside, refinement meets functionality — offering comfort, space, and cutting-edge technology. Effortless on long journeys yet ferocious on the open road, the GTS transforms versatility into an art form.

Beyond the Journey

Designed to turn every road into an experience of style and performance.

Effortless Speed

The GTS surges from 0 to 100 km/h in just 3.2 seconds, delivering supercar performance with grace. Its twin-turbo V8 channels raw power into seamless acceleration, making every stretch of road an invitation to experience velocity with composure and control.

Grand Touring Interior

Step inside the GTS and discover a world where luxury meets performance. Soft-touch materials, premium finishes, and intuitive controls create an atmosphere of refined comfort. Designed for long journeys yet infused with McLaren’s racing DNA, the interior blends elegance with driver-focused precision.

Adaptive Aero Dynamics

The W1’s rear spoiler automatically rises at high speeds to increase downforce, enhancing stability and cornering precision. At lower speeds, it retracts smoothly to maintain sleek aerodynamics and a refined profile. This dynamic system ensures optimal performance and control in every driving condition.
