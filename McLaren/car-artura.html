UNVEILING ARTURA

McLaren’s cutting-edge hybrid supercar, engineered for exhilarating performance and efficiency. Born from relentless innovation and inspired by the racetrack, the Artura blends electrified power with precision handling. Every curve, every surface, is designed to optimize aerodynamics and responsiveness. Lighter, sharper, and more agile, the Artura represents the next evolution of McLaren’s performance philosophy

Efficiency meets power

Hybrid technology, unleashed with McLaren precision.

Electrified Performance

From 0 to 100 km/h in just 3.0 seconds, the Artura fuses electric torque with a twin-turbo V6 for instant response. The seamless hybrid system delivers explosive acceleration while maintaining remarkable efficiency — proof that supercar thrills and future-ready technology can coexist.

Inside the Future

Step inside and discover a fusion of handcrafted luxury and cutting-edge innovation. Premium materials, refined stitching, and sculpted ergonomics are paired with advanced digital displays and seamless smartphone integration.

Innovative Hybrid Powertrain

At its core lies a revolutionary twin-turbo V6 paired with an advanced E-motor. Together, they unleash 690 horsepower with uncompromising precision. Light, compact, and efficient, this powertrain redefines what it means to drive a hybrid supercar.
